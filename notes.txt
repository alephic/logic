Apply:

  func arg
  f x
  f x y
  f (g x) y

Symbolic constants behave as constructors when applied
Lambdas behave as functions when applied
Lambda:

  <$arg> body
  <$x> y
  <$arg: constraint> body
  <$x: c x y> z
  <$x> <$y> f x y

Declare:

  {declaration} body
  {c x y} f x

Query:

  [val: constraint]
  [x: c $x]
